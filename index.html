About Me

I am an RTL Design Engineer at SiFive. I studied Computer Engineering at Dartmouth College with an emphasis on digital design. I finished there in March 2023 with a B.A. in Engineering Sciences modified with Computer Science, along with a B.E. with Computer Engineering.

Previously, I worked as a Digital Design Engineer at Analog Devices starting in April 2023. I worked on the DSP core in the ADEMA124/7. I then moved to the pin electronics team and worked on a digital timing solution for pin electronics.

After a few years at college and some time at ADI and SiFive, I've gained proficiency in:

  • Digital Design
    • SystemVerilog
    • Chisel
  • Design Verification
  • Cadence/Synopsys tools
